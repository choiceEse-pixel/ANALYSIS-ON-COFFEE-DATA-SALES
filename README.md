# ANALYSIS-ON-COFFEE-DATA-SALES
## PROJECT DESCRIPTION
This analysis focuses on evaluating transactional coffee sales data.It explores sales patterns, customer preferences, and revenue trends within a coffee business. This project uncovers insights  that guide business decisions such as pricing, inventory and marketing strategies. The data captures detailed information about what customers buy, when they buy it, and how much revenue is generated. 
### BUSINESS OVERVIEW
 Jerome Coffee Company operates within the fast growing beverage industry, Retail and wholesale distribution of coffee products (beans, ground coffee, ready-to-drink beverages), Monitoring daily sales transactions across multiple outlets or channels, Managing inventory and supply chain efficiency,Collecting and analyzing customer and sales data. 
### AIM OF PROJECT
The major aim of this project is to analyze coffee sales data to uncover meaningful insights that support business decision-making, identify key trends,improve sales performance, and enhance customer satisfaction. this project aims to:
- Analyze sales performance by evaluating total revenue and sales trends
-  Analyze product performance by determining the best-selling and least-selling coffee products.
-  Identify Customer Purchasing Patterns by analyzing customer buying behavior and preferences and also identify peak purchasing times, days, and seasons.
-  Assess Revenue Drivers by determining which products contribute the most to overall revenue. Evaluate the relationship between product pricing and sales volume.
-  Examine Temporal Trends by investigating daily, weekly, monthly, and seasonal sales patterns.
 ### DATASET DESCRIPTION
 The Jerome Coffee Sales dataset contains transactional records from a coffee shop business, capturing details of products sold, sales quantities, pricing, transaction dates, and revenue generated. The dataset is designed to support sales performance analysis, customer purchasing behavior studies.
The dataset supports business intelligence tasks such as:

Revenue analysis
Customer behavior analysis
Product performance evaluation
Time-series (monthly & hourly) analysis
